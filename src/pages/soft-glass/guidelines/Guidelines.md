# Soft Glass Design System

## Philosophy
Soft Glass (Frosted Clay) combines the tactile, friendly nature of Claymorphism with the modern, depth-rich aesthetic of Glassmorphism.

## Principles
1. **Softness**: Rounded corners (20px+), soft shadows, pastel colors.
2. **Depth**: Use layers of translucency (Glass) and inner shadows (Clay) to create hierarchy.
3. **Simplicity**: Avoid harsh borders or high contrast strokes.

## Usage
- **Panels**: Use `.glass-panel` class.
- **Buttons**: Use `Button` component with standard variants.
- **Inputs**: `Input` component comes with built-in inner shadow.
